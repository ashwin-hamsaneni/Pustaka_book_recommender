Ranking Metrics Breakdown
NDCG@K — Ranking Quality
Normalised Discounted Cumulative Gain — rewards correct items appearing higher in the list.
Recall@K — Coverage of Relevant Items
Fraction of books the user actually interacted with that appear in the top-K recommendations.
MAP@K — Mean Average Precision
Precision averaged across all recall levels — sensitive to position of relevant items.
Precision@K — Hit Rate
Fraction of the top-K recommended books that are actually relevant to the user.
